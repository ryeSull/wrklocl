About

People don't work the way they used to. Technology has provided flexibility and created a mobile workforce that no longer needs to be tethered to a traditional office. At the same time, this new breed of worker still needs business resources and an environment in which to be professional and productive. Work Station is a member based coworking location with a variety of work environments to suit just about every working style. We have a full complement of office amenities, and provide a flexible alternative to working in a home office, coffee shop or expensive long-term  office rental. We have created a professional setting where you can work, and can have as much or as little interaction with fellow members as you wish. Whether you are a solo entrepreneur, part of a start-up, studying for a licensing exam, a remote worker for a large corporation, or even if you are conducting a job search, we can help you Work, Your Way.
